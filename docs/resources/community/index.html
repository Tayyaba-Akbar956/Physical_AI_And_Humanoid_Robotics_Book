Community: Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

This section provides information about communities, forums, organizations, and resources where you can connect with other Physical AI and humanoid robotics practitioners, get help, share knowledge, and collaborate on projects.

Learning and Support Communities​

Official ROS Community​

  1. ROS Discourse Forum (https://discourse.ros.org/)

    • The primary discussion forum for the Robot Operating System
    • Q&A sections for troubleshooting and best practices
    • Announcements about new releases and features
    • Community showcase for projects and applications

  2. ROS Answers (https://answers.ros.org/)

    • Question and answer platform for ROS users
    • Searchable database of common issues and solutions
    • Community-driven problem solving
    • Good place to ask specific technical questions

  3. ROS Wiki (http://wiki.ros.org/)

    • Comprehensive collection of ROS documentation
    • Tutorials and user guides
    • Package documentation and APIs
    • Community-contributed resources

Gazebo Community​

  1. Gazebo Simulation Community (https://community.gazebosim.org/)

    • Discussion forum for Gazebo users and developers
    • Q&A for simulation setup and troubleshooting
    • Announcements about new releases and features
    • Community examples and tutorials

  2. Gazebo Answers (https://answers.gazebosim.org/)

    • Question and answer platform for Gazebo simulation
    • Searchable database of common simulation issues
    • Best practices for physics simulation

NVIDIA Isaac Community​

  1. NVIDIA Developer Forums - Robotics (https://forums.developer.nvidia.com/c/ai-on-rtos/robotics/)

    • Community for Isaac Sim, Isaac ROS, and robotics applications
    • Technical support from NVIDIA engineers
    • Sample code and projects
    • Discussion about GPU-accelerated robotics

  2. Isaac ROS GitHub Organization (https://github.com/NVIDIA-ISAAC-ROS)

    • Open-source ROS packages optimized for NVIDIA hardware
    • Issue tracking and community contributions
    • Example applications and tutorials
    • Technical documentation and API references

Academic and Research Communities​

Conferences and Workshops​

  1. IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA)

    • Annual premier robotics conference
    • Research paper presentations and networking opportunities
    • Online proceedings and video archives
    • Student competitions and workshops

  2. IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS)

    • Major international robotics conference
    • Comprehensive coverage of robotics research
    • Workshops and tutorials on specialized topics
    • Networking with industry and academic professionals

  3. Robotics: Science and Systems (RSS)

    • High-quality robotics conference
    • Focused on scientific foundations
    • Workshop and tutorial sessions
    • Open discussion and collaboration format

Research Organizations​

  1. IEEE Robotics and Automation Society (RAS)

    • Professional organization for robotics professionals
    • Publications, conferences, and networking
    • Technical committees on specialized topics
    • Student branch support and scholarships
    • Website: https://www.ieee-ras.org/

  2. International Federation of Robotics (IFR)

    • Global robotics organization
    • Industry reports and market analysis
    • World Robotics Congress
    • Standards and ethics initiatives
    • Website: https://ifr.org/

Academic Journals and Publications​

  1. The International Journal of Robotics Research (IJRR)

    • Premier robotics research journal
    • High-quality peer-reviewed articles
    • Special issues on specific topics
    • Review articles summarizing state of the art

  2. IEEE Transactions on Robotics (T-RO)

    • Leading robotics research publication
    • Technical papers on all aspects of robotics
    • Rapid publication for significant contributions

Online Communities and Social Platforms​

Reddit Communities​

  1. r/Robotics (https://www.reddit.com/r/Robotics/)

    • General robotics discussion
    • Project showcases and feedback
    • News and announcements
    • Beginner-friendly discussions

  2. r/HumanoidRobotics (https://www.reddit.com/r/HumanoidRobotics/)

    • Specific focus on humanoid robotics
    • Project discussions and tutorials
    • Hardware reviews and recommendations
    • Community collaboration

Discord and Slack Groups​

  1. Robotics Discord Server

    • Real-time chat and collaboration
    • Specialized channels for different topics
    • Project showcases and feedback
    • Code help and troubleshooting

  2. Open Robotics Community Slack

    • Real-time community for ROS and Gazebo users
    • Direct access to developers and maintainers
    • Project collaboration channels
    • Office hours and Q&A sessions

GitHub Organizations and Projects​

  1. ROS Industrial (https://github.com/ros-industrial)

    • Industrial robotics applications of ROS
    • Support packages for commercial robots
    • Community guidelines and best practices
    • Collaboration on industrial solutions

  2. PickNik Robotics (https://github.com/PickNikRobotics)

    • ROS consulting and development
    • Open-source tools and utilities
    • Tutorials and example projects
    • Community-focused development

Professional Networks and Organizations​

Industry Groups​

  1. Robotic Industries Association (RIA)

    • North American robotics industry organization
    • Standards and best practices
    • Industry reports and market analysis
    • Robotic Industries Conference and exhibitions
    • Website: https://www.robotics.org/

  2. European Robotics Platform (euRobotics)

    • European robotics research and innovation
    • Collaboration between academia and industry
    • Funding opportunities and project partnerships
    • Technical working groups and standards
    • Website: https://www.euronrobotics.eu/

Professional Development​

  1. ACM Special Interest Group on Computer Graphics (SIGGRAPH)

    • For those interested in simulation graphics and visualization
    • Annual conference with robotics applications
    • Technical papers on rendering and visualization
    • Website: https://www.siggraph.org/

  2. Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence (AAAI)

    • AI-focused organization with robotics applications
    • Annual conference and specialized workshops
    • Publications and research resources
    • Website: https://www.aaai.org/

Local Communities and Maker Spaces​

Robotics Meetups and Groups​

  1. RoboCup Communities

    • International robotics competition communities
    • Local leagues and training groups
    • Educational initiatives and workshops
    • Focus on humanoid, soccer, rescue, and other robotics challenges

  2. FIRST Robotics Teams

    • Educational robotics programs
    • Mentorship and learning opportunities
    • Community outreach and education
    • STEM education initiatives

Makerspaces and Hackerspaces​

  1. Local Makerspaces
    • Physical spaces for robotics prototyping and testing
    • Shared tools and equipment
    • Community workshops and learning sessions
    • Collaboration opportunities
    • Find local spaces through https://makerspaces.com/

    • University-level robotics education
    • Community forums for discussion
    • Hands-on projects and assignments
    • Professional certificates available

YouTube Channels and Content Creators​

  1. ROS Official Channel

    • Tutorials and demonstrations
    • Updates and announcements
    • Community showcases
    • Technical presentations

  2. The Construct (ROS)

    • Comprehensive ROS tutorials
    • Simulation and practical examples
    • Community Q&A sessions
    • Online courses and certification

  3. Articulated Robotics

    • Mechanical engineering and robotics education

    • Control theory explanations
    • Community engagement and feedback

    • Code contribution process
    • Community guidelines

Project Showcases​

  1. ROS Projects Showcase

    • Community-driven project gallery
    • Documentation and tutorials
    • Collaboration opportunities
    • Inspiration for new projects

  2. Hackaday Robotics (https://hackaday.com/tag/robotics/)

    • DIY and maker robotics projects
    • Hardware and software tutorials
    • Community feedback and suggestions
    • Inspiration and innovation

    • Technical presentations and tutorials
    • Networking and collaboration opportunities
    • Community project showcases

  2. RoboBusiness

    • Business and commercial applications of robotics
    • Industry trends and market analysis

Virtual Events and Webinars​

    • Technical sessions and workshops
    • AI and robotics integration topics

    • Community-driven answers
    • Cross-referenced with other technologies

  2. ROS Debugging Guide

    • Common issues and solutions
    • Diagnostic tools and techniques
    • Community-provided solutions

  2. Women in Robotics

    • Support and networking for women in robotics
    • Mentorship programs

    • Community and collaboration opportunities

Contributing to the Community​

Sharing Knowledge​

  1. Write Technical Blogs

    • Share project experiences and learnings
    • Document problem-solving approaches
    • Contribute to community knowledge base
    • Build professional reputation and network

  2. Create Tutorials and Documentation

    • Help other learners with resources
    • Improve open-source documentation
    • Contribute to community education
    • Practice technical writing skills

Project Collaboration​

  1. GitHub Collaboration

    • Contribute to open-source projects
    • Fork and improve existing code
    • Submit pull requests with improvements
    • Report issues and suggest features

  2. ROS Community Projects

    • Participate in collaborative development
    • Join working groups and special interest groups
    • Contribute to standards and best practices
    • Mentor newcomers to the field

Key Takeaways​

  • Multiple communities exist for different aspects of Physical AI development
  • Academic communities focus on research and scientific foundations
  • Professional organizations provide industry resources and standards
  • Online platforms offer real-time support and collaboration
  • Local communities provide hands-on learning opportunities
  • Contributing to communities enhances your own learning
  • Networking through communities opens career opportunities

Best Practices for Community Engagement​

  1. Read community guidelines before posting
  2. Search existing resources before asking questions
  3. Ask specific, well-formatted questions with sufficient context
  4. Provide helpful answers when you can assist others
  5. Share your knowledge through tutorials, documentation, or projects
  6. Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
  7. Give back to the community that has helped you learn

Next Steps​

Now that you've explored the available community resources, begin engaging with the community that best matches your current needs and interests. Whether you need technical support, want to learn from others' experiences, or have knowledge to share, there's a community ready to welcome you.
